You may know of ultrasound from its prevalence in prenatal care. Beyond the uterus, it is used to image the heart, vascular system, brain, and abdomen. The ways in which it is used are expanding rapidly from prevention to detection to procedure image guidance, procedure follow-up, and chronic care. Once a tool relegated primarily to the hospital, the number of locations in which ultrasound can now be used is expanding greatly, including everywhere from the clinic to the battlefield to the procedure room to the pharmacy.

TeleHealthRobotics uses TRUDI: Tele-Robotic Ultrasound for Distance Imaging. TRUDI leverages the convergence of low cost robotics and control systems with advanced tele-presence. It allows an experienced operator located anywhere in the world to operate a diagnostic ultrasound system at a distance.
